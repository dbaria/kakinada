A world class greenfield port in Andhra Pradesh's Kakinada district, which is expected to support a future business revolution. With the help of its advanced technology, it offers cost effective services as well as an infrastructure prepared to serve as India's eastern gateway.

India’s gateway to east coast offering the most innovative facilities and services

An Indian private company, Kakinada Gateway Port Limited (KGPL), is working to build an all-weather, multi-cargo, deep-water port in the Andhra Pradesh district of Kakinada.

Kakinada is also known as smart city strategically located on the east coast, in a region with abundant natural gas and oil reserves. The port runs on cutting-edge technology and has excellent logistical connections to roads, railroads, airports and seaports.

ABOUT KAKINADA Gateway PORT

Smarter Infrastructure, rich culture - facilitating evolution

Along the Andhra Pradesh coast, Kakinada SEZ Ltd is promoting the Kakinada Gateway Port development. The project, which spans over 1650 acres, is situated near to the midst of the east coast and boasts a plethora of industrial, port and people potential. With three berths plus one and a 17.5-meter draft, this port can handle 16 million tonnes of general cargo, including liquid cargo, bulk cargo, chemicals and coal.

Why choose Kakinada Gateway Port?

Kakinada Gateway Port aims to provide superior quality services with higher reliability at competitive prices. The port offers ease of transportation as it is enabled with multiple lanes of entrance and the road for entry and exit of trucks at port entrance is directly connecting to National Highways. There are many additional advantages that aids the whole process and makes the execution smooth. A few of these advantages are:

  • Truck availability
  • Security of the cargo against pilferage
  • Instant loading and unloading of the trucks
  • Ample yard space for cargo
  • Connectivity to the highways

All Weather Deep Sea Commercial Sea Port

  1. Phase 01 - Managing Port

    An all-weather deep water Sea Port, the Kakinada Gateway Port will be situated on the Eastern Coast of India. It is being developed adjacent to the Industrial City of Kakinada district in Andhra Pradesh. It is a Greenfield Commercial Port, which is expected to serve as a multi-cargo and deep-draft port as well. An overview of the deep draft- the Kakinada Gateway Port will be equipped with one of the deepest drafts on the entire east coast of India. Further, the Government of India has granted the “Environmental Clearance and Consent for the Establishment for Phase-1” of this Port.
